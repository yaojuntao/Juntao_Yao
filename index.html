About Me

Juntao Yao is a PhD candidate student under the supervision of Prof. Shuo Wang, at University of Florida. His research interests include power electronics, electromagnetic interference (EMI), and magnetic component design. He received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in electrical engineering from Wuhan University, Wuhan, China, in 2013 and 2016, respectively. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in the Power Electronics and Electrical Power Research Laboratory (PEEPRL), University of Florida, Gainesville, FL, USA.
